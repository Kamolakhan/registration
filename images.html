Places to visit

Samarkand - historic city on the Silk Road

Bukhara - ancient center of trade and culture

What to try

Plov - traditional rice dish with meat and spices

Lagman - hand-pulled noodles with meat and vegetables

Kazan-kabob - meat and potatoes cooked in a cauldron

Narin - thinly sliced noodles with horse meat

Manti - steamed dumplings filled with meat and onions

Samsa - baked pastry with meat filling
